# DMCA Policy & Copyright Notice - Rejection Hero

**Last Updated: [DATE]**

## Copyright Protection

Rejection Hero respects intellectual property rights and expects our users to do the same. This policy outlines our procedures for handling copyright infringement claims under the Digital Millennium Copyright Act (DMCA).

## Reporting Copyright Infringement

If you believe your copyrighted work has been used on Rejection Hero without authorization, you may submit a DMCA takedown notice.

### Required Information:

1. **Identification of Copyrighted Work**
   - Description of the copyrighted work
   - Location of the original work (URL, publication, etc.)
   - Registration number (if applicable)

2. **Identification of Infringing Material**
   - Specific location of infringing content on Rejection Hero
   - Screenshots or links to the content
   - User information (if known)

3. **Contact Information**
   - Your full name
   - Mailing address
   - Phone number
   - Email address

4. **Statement of Good Faith**
   - Statement that you believe the use is not authorized
   - Statement that the information is accurate
   - Statement that you are the copyright owner or authorized agent

5. **Signature**
   - Physical or electronic signature

### Submit DMCA Notice To:

**Email**: copyright@rejectionhero.com  
**Subject**: DMCA Takedown Notice

**Or Mail To**:
```
Rejection Hero
Attn: DMCA Agent
[COMPANY ADDRESS]
```

## Counter-Notification

If you believe your content was removed in error, you may submit a counter-notification.

### Required Information:

1. **Identification of Removed Content**
   - Description of removed content
   - Location before removal

2. **Statement of Good Faith**
   - Statement that you believe the removal was a mistake
   - Statement that you consent to jurisdiction

3. **Contact Information**
   - Full name, address, phone, email

4. **Signature**
   - Physical or electronic signature

### Submit Counter-Notification To:

**Email**: copyright@rejectionhero.com  
**Subject**: DMCA Counter-Notification

## Our Process

1. **Review**: We review all DMCA notices promptly
2. **Removal**: If valid, we remove or disable access to infringing content
3. **Notification**: We notify the user who posted the content
4. **Counter-Notification**: User may submit a counter-notification
5. **Restoration**: If counter-notification is valid, content may be restored after 10 business days

## Repeat Infringers

Users who repeatedly infringe copyrights may have their accounts terminated.

## False Claims

Knowingly submitting false DMCA notices may result in legal liability. Only submit notices for content you genuinely believe infringes your copyright.

## User-Generated Content

Users are responsible for ensuring they have rights to content they post. By posting content, users represent they have necessary rights or permissions.

## Fair Use

We respect fair use and other copyright exceptions. Counter-notifications may include fair use arguments.
