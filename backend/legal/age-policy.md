# Age Verification Policy - Rejection Hero

**Last Updated: [DATE]**

## Age Requirements

Rejection Hero has different age requirements for different features:

### Minimum Age: 13 Years Old
- **COPPA Compliance**: Users must be at least 13 to use the App
- **Basic Features**: Quest creation, tracking, social features
- **Parental Consent**: Users aged 13-17 require parental consent

### Live Streaming: 18 Years Old
- **Age Requirement**: Must be 18+ to use live streaming features
- **Reason**: Live streaming involves recording and public exposure
- **Verification**: Age will be verified before enabling streaming

## Age Verification Process

### During Registration:
1. **Age Question**: Users are asked their age before downloading/using the App
2. **Verification**: Age is verified during account creation
3. **Restrictions**: Features are restricted based on age
4. **Parental Consent**: Users 13-17 must provide parental consent

### Ongoing Verification:
- **Account Review**: We may request additional verification
- **Feature Access**: Age-restricted features require verification
- **Suspension**: Accounts may be suspended if age is misrepresented

## Parental Consent (Users 13-17)

### Requirements:
- **Parent/Guardian Consent**: Must be obtained before account activation
- **Verification**: Parent/guardian must verify their identity
- **Ongoing Supervision**: Parents should monitor their child's use
- **Contact**: Parents can contact us at privacy@rejectionhero.com

### Process:
1. User indicates they are 13-17
2. Parent/guardian contact information is collected
3. Parent/guardian receives consent form via email
4. Parent/guardian must sign and return consent
5. Account is activated after consent is received

## Restricted Features by Age

### Users 13-17:
- ✅ Basic quest features
- ✅ Social features (with restrictions)
- ✅ Messaging (with safety features)
- ❌ Live streaming
- ❌ Certain mature content
- ❌ Payment features (may require parent)

### Users 18+:
- ✅ All features available
- ✅ Live streaming
- ✅ Full social features
- ✅ Payment and subscription features

## Reporting Underage Users

If you believe a user is underage:
- **Report in App**: Use the report feature
- **Email**: safety@rejectionhero.com
- **Include**: User information and evidence

We investigate all reports and may suspend accounts if age is misrepresented.

## Account Termination

Accounts may be terminated if:
- User is found to be under 13
- User misrepresents their age
- Parental consent is not obtained (for 13-17)
- User violates age-related restrictions

## Data Protection for Minors

### COPPA Compliance:
- **No Collection**: We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13
- **Parental Rights**: Parents may review, delete, or modify their child's data
- **Limited Data**: We collect minimal data necessary for app functionality
- **No Marketing**: We do not market to children

### GDPR (EU):
- **Age of Consent**: Varies by country (typically 13-16)
- **Parental Consent**: Required for users below age of consent
- **Data Rights**: Parents may exercise data rights on behalf of children

## Safety Features for Minors

### Enhanced Protection:
- **Content Filtering**: Stricter content moderation
- **Privacy Settings**: Enhanced privacy by default
- **Reporting**: Easy reporting of inappropriate content
- **Parental Controls**: Parents can manage account settings
