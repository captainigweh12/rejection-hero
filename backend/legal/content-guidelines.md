## ‚ùå Prohibited Content and Behavior

### 1. Harassment and Bullying
- **No harassment**: Targeting, stalking, or intimidating others
- **No bullying**: Repeated negative behavior toward individuals
- **No threats**: Threats of violence, harm, or illegal activity
- **No doxxing**: Sharing private information without consent

### 2. Hate Speech and Discrimination
- **No hate speech**: Content attacking groups based on race, religion, gender, sexual orientation, etc.
- **No discrimination**: Discriminatory language or behavior
- **No extremism**: Content promoting violence or extremism

### 3. Sexual Content
- **No nudity**: Explicit sexual content or nudity
- **No sexual harassment**: Unwanted sexual advances or content
- **No adult content**: Pornographic or sexually explicit material

### 4. Dangerous Challenges
- **No physical harm**: Quests that could cause injury
- **No illegal activities**: Challenges involving illegal acts
- **No self-harm**: Content promoting self-harm or suicide
- **No dangerous stunts**: Activities that put you or others at risk

### 5. Fraud and Deception
- **No false submissions**: Fake quest completions or fraudulent "No" counts
- **No scams**: Deceptive practices or fraud
- **No impersonation**: Pretending to be someone else
- **No spam**: Repetitive or unwanted content

### 6. Copyright and Intellectual Property
- **No copyright infringement**: Using others' content without permission
- **No trademark violations**: Using protected trademarks
- **Respect DMCA**: Follow our DMCA policy (see separate document)

### 7. Recording Without Consent
- **Follow local laws**: One-party vs two-party consent varies by location
- **Get permission**: Always obtain consent before recording others
- **Respect privacy**: Do not record private individuals without permission
- **You are responsible**: We are not liable for recording law violations

### 8. Spam and Misuse
- **No spam**: Repetitive, unwanted, or promotional content
- **No manipulation**: Gaming the system or exploiting features
- **No unauthorized access**: Attempting to hack or breach security
