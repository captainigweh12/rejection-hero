# Privacy Policy - Rejection Hero

**Last Updated: [DATE]**

## 1. Introduction

Rejection Hero ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application ("App").

## 2. Information We Collect

### Personal Information:
- **Account Information**: Name, email address, username, profile picture
- **Authentication Data**: Login credentials, OAuth tokens (Google)
- **Profile Data**: Interests, goals, user context, bio
- **Usage Data**: App interactions, quest completions, progress tracking
- **Device Information**: Device type, operating system, unique device identifiers
- **Location Data**: If you choose to use location-based features

### Content You Create:
- Quest submissions and recordings
- Live stream content
- Posts, comments, and messages
- Stories and moments
- Journal entries
- Audio recordings and transcriptions

### Automatically Collected:
- Analytics data (app usage, features used)
- Crash reports and error logs
- IP address and network information
- Cookies and similar tracking technologies

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:
- Provide and improve the App
- Process your account and transactions
- Generate personalized quests using AI
- Enable social features (messaging, groups, live streaming)
- Send notifications and updates
- Analyze usage patterns
- Ensure safety and security
- Comply with legal obligations

## 4. Data Storage and Security

- **Storage**: Data is stored securely using industry-standard encryption
- **Database**: User data stored in secure databases (SQLite/PostgreSQL)
- **Third-Party Services**: We use trusted services (Supabase, Stripe, OpenAI, etc.)
- **Security Measures**: Encryption, secure authentication, regular security audits
- **Data Retention**: We retain data as long as your account is active or as required by law

## 5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may collect data:

- **Authentication**: Better Auth (session management)
- **Database**: Supabase/Prisma (data storage)
- **AI Services**: OpenAI (quest generation), Perplexity AI
- **Payments**: Stripe (subscription and payment processing)
- **Email**: Resend (email delivery)
- **Analytics**: App usage analytics
- **Push Notifications**: Expo Notifications
- **Live Streaming**: Daily.co (WebRTC services)
- **CRM**: GoHighLevel (contact management)

Each service has its own privacy policy. We encourage you to review them.

## 6. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do NOT sell your personal information. We may share data:
- **With Your Consent**: When you explicitly agree
- **Service Providers**: Trusted third parties who assist in operating the App
- **Legal Requirements**: When required by law or to protect rights
- **Business Transfers**: In connection with mergers or acquisitions
- **Public Content**: Content you post publicly (posts, streams) is visible to others
