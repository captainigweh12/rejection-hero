## Auto-Renewal

### How It Works:
- **Automatic Renewal**: Your subscription renews automatically each month
- **Billing Date**: You are charged on the same date each month
- **Notification**: You receive email notification before renewal
- **No Refund**: Charges are non-refundable after renewal

### Canceling Auto-Renewal:
1. **In App**: Go to Settings → Subscriptions → Cancel
2. **Email**: Contact support@rejectionhero.com
3. **Timing**: Cancel at least 24 hours before renewal date
4. **Access**: You retain access until the end of the current period
