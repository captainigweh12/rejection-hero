# Safety & Misconduct Reporting Policy - Rejection Hero

**Last Updated: [DATE]**

## Our Commitment to Safety

Rejection Hero is committed to maintaining a safe and respectful community. This policy outlines how to report misconduct and how we handle safety concerns.

## What to Report

### Immediate Safety Concerns:
- **Threats of Violence**: Direct or indirect threats
- **Self-Harm**: Content promoting self-harm or suicide
- **Illegal Activities**: Criminal behavior or illegal content
- **Dangerous Challenges**: Quests that could cause harm
- **Harassment**: Persistent harassment or bullying
- **Stalking**: Unwanted following or monitoring

### Community Violations:
- **Harassment**: Bullying, intimidation, or targeting
- **Hate Speech**: Discriminatory or hateful content
- **Spam**: Repetitive or unwanted content
- **Fraud**: Scams or deceptive practices
- **Copyright**: Infringement of intellectual property
- **Inappropriate Content**: Sexual, violent, or offensive content

## How to Report

### In-App Reporting:
1. **Content Reports**: Tap "Report" on posts, comments, or streams
2. **User Reports**: Report users from their profile
3. **Quest Reports**: Report inappropriate or dangerous quests
4. **Quick Access**: Report button available throughout the App

### Email Reporting:
- **General Reports**: safety@rejectionhero.com
- **Urgent Safety**: urgent@rejectionhero.com (for immediate threats)
- **Harassment**: harassment@rejectionhero.com
- **Include**: Screenshots, user information, description, timestamps

### What to Include:
- **Screenshots**: Visual evidence of the violation
- **User Information**: Username, profile link, or user ID
- **Description**: Detailed description of the incident
- **Timestamps**: When the incident occurred
- **Context**: Any relevant background information

## Our Investigation Process

### Review Timeline:
- **Urgent Safety**: Reviewed within 24 hours
- **Harassment**: Reviewed within 48 hours
- **General Reports**: Reviewed within 72 hours
- **Complex Cases**: May take up to 7 days

### Investigation Steps:
1. **Initial Review**: Automated and human review
2. **Evidence Collection**: Gather relevant information
3. **User Notification**: Notify reported user (if applicable)
4. **Decision**: Determine appropriate action
5. **Action Taken**: Implement consequences
6. **Follow-Up**: Monitor and follow up as needed

## Actions We Take

### Warning:
- **First Violation**: Minor violations receive a warning
- **Education**: Information about community guidelines
- **No Action**: Warning only, no restrictions

### Content Removal:
- **Violating Content**: Removed immediately
- **Notification**: User notified of removal
- **Appeal**: User may appeal removal

### Temporary Suspension:
- **Repeated Violations**: Account suspended for a period
- **Duration**: 1 day to 30 days depending on severity
- **Appeal**: User may appeal suspension

### Permanent Ban:
- **Severe Violations**: Immediate permanent ban
- **Examples**: Threats, illegal activity, repeated harassment
- **Appeal**: Limited appeal process for permanent bans

### Legal Action:
- **Criminal Activity**: Reported to law enforcement
- **Legal Violations**: May pursue legal action
- **Cooperation**: We cooperate with law enforcement

## Appeals Process

### How to Appeal:
1. **Submit Appeal**: Email appeals@rejectionhero.com
2. **Include**: Account information, reason for appeal, evidence
3. **Review**: Appeals reviewed within 7 days
4. **Decision**: Final decision communicated via email

### Appeal Criteria:
- **New Evidence**: Information not previously considered
- **Mistaken Identity**: Wrong user was penalized
- **Policy Change**: Action taken under old policy
- **Extenuating Circumstances**: Special circumstances

## False Reports

### Consequences:
- **False Reports**: Knowingly false reports may result in action
- **Abuse of System**: Repeated false reports may lead to suspension
- **Legal Liability**: False reports may have legal consequences

## Confidentiality

### We Protect:
- **Reporter Identity**: We protect reporter identity when possible
- **Investigation Details**: Investigation details are confidential
- **User Privacy**: We respect user privacy while investigating

### We May Disclose:
- **Legal Requirements**: When required by law
- **Safety Concerns**: To protect user safety
- **Law Enforcement**: When cooperating with authorities
