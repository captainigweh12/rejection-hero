# Payment Policy

**Effective Date:** November 18, 2025

## 1. Overview

This Payment Policy governs all transactions within the Rejection Hero App, including in-app purchases, subscriptions, and token purchases.

## 2. Payment Methods

We accept the following payment methods:
- Credit/Debit Cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express)
- Digital wallets (Apple Pay, Google Pay)
- PayPal
- Other approved payment processors

All payments are processed by secure third-party providers using encryption technology.

## 3. In-App Purchases

### 3.1 Tokens and Virtual Currency
- Tokens are virtual currency used to send quests to friends
- Tokens have no real-world cash value
- Token purchases are non-refundable except as required by law
- Exchange rates are displayed before purchase
- Tokens do not expire unless stated otherwise

### 3.2 Pricing
- All prices are in USD unless otherwise specified
- Prices may vary by location and applicable taxes
- We reserve the right to change prices with 30 days notice
- Local taxes will be added to your total

## 4. Subscriptions

### 4.1 Premium Features
- Optional paid subscriptions unlock premium features
- Subscriptions auto-renew unless cancelled
- You will be notified of renewal 24 hours before billing
- You can cancel anytime through App settings or your account

### 4.2 Billing Cycle
- Subscriptions are billed monthly or annually
- Billing occurs on the same date each period
- Unused portions of free trials cannot be refunded
- Refunds for paid subscription periods are not provided, except as required by law

## 5. Refund Policy

### 5.1 Refundable Items
- Accidental purchases within 24 hours
- Duplicate charges
- Fraudulent transactions

### 5.2 Refund Process
1. Submit a refund request with your order ID
2. Provide reason for refund
3. Allow 5-7 business days for processing
4. Refunds are issued to original payment method

### 5.3 Non-Refundable Items
- Tokens and virtual currency (unless charged in error)
- Completed subscriptions
- Expired offers or promotions

## 6. Billing Disputes

For billing disputes, contact us within 30 days of the transaction:
Email: billing@rejectionhero.com

Include:
- Order number
- Transaction date
- Amount disputed
- Reason for dispute

## 7. Failed Payments

If a payment fails:
- You will receive notification
- Your account access may be temporarily limited
- You will have 7 days to update payment information
- Account may be suspended if payment remains unpaid

## 8. Fraud Prevention

We employ fraud detection systems to protect against:
- Unauthorized purchases
- Chargebacks and disputes
- Duplicate transactions
- Suspicious activity

Fraudulent accounts may be terminated and reported to authorities.

## 9. Third-Party Payment Processors

Payment processing is handled by third parties who comply with:
- PCI DSS standards
- GDPR and privacy regulations
- Industry security standards

We do not store full credit card information.

## 10. Tax Responsibility

Consumers are responsible for any local taxes on purchases. We comply with applicable tax laws and may collect taxes on your behalf.

## 11. Currency Conversion

For international transactions, currency conversion may apply. Exchange rates are provided by our payment processor and may differ from rates shown elsewhere.

## 12. Pricing Changes

We may change subscription pricing with 30 days notice. Price changes apply to new subscriptions only unless renewal occurs.

## 13. Promotional Offers

- Promotional codes cannot be combined
- Offers are non-transferable
- Expired codes are not valid
- Unused promotional credit expires after 90 days

## 14. Account Deletion and Refunds

- Cancelling your account does not automatically cancel subscriptions
- You must cancel subscriptions separately
- Outstanding payments remain due
- No refunds for cancelled accounts

## 15. Contact

For payment-related questions:
Email: billing@rejectionhero.com
