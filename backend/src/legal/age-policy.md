# Age Verification Policy

**Effective Date:** November 18, 2025

## 1. Age Requirement

Rejection Hero is designed for users aged 13 and above. Our service is divided into age-appropriate experiences:

- **Ages 13-17:** Full access with parental consent features
- **Ages 18+:** Unrestricted access

## 2. Age Verification Process

To ensure a safe community, we require age verification through one of the following methods:
- Birth date provided during account creation
- Government-issued ID verification
- Third-party age verification services

## 3. Parental Consent for Minors (Ages 13-17)

If you are between 13-17 years old, we require parental or guardian consent for:
- Account creation and usage
- Engagement in live streaming activities
- Participation in community challenges
- Access to user-generated content

Parents/guardians can:
- Monitor their child's activity
- Set usage limits
- Review and manage accepted policies
- Request account deletion

## 4. Data Collection for Age Verification

To verify age, we collect:
- Date of birth
- Government-issued ID information (if applicable)
- Parental email and consent records

This information is stored securely and used solely for age verification purposes.

## 5. Restrictions for Minors

Users under 18 are prohibited from:
- Accessing adult-oriented content or challenges
- Engaging in certain high-risk quest categories
- Monetizing their account or making purchases without parental consent
- Accessing live streaming features without parental approval

## 6. False Age Declaration

Misrepresenting your age is a violation of our Terms of Service. Accounts found to have provided false age information will be subject to termination.

## 7. Age Verification Failures

If you fail age verification, you will not be able to:
- Create or access an account
- Participate in community features
- Access the full App experience

You may re-attempt verification or contact support@rejectionhero.com for assistance.

## 8. Updates to Age Status

As you age into different categories (e.g., turning 18), your account permissions will automatically update.

## 9. Parental Controls

We provide tools for parents/guardians to manage their minor children's accounts, including activity monitoring and usage limits.

## 10. Privacy Protection

All age verification data is protected with industry-standard encryption and is not shared with third parties except as required by law.
