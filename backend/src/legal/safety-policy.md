# Safety & Misconduct Reporting Policy

**Effective Date:** November 18, 2025

## 1. Commitment to Safety

Rejection Hero is committed to maintaining a safe community environment. We take all safety concerns seriously and investigate reports promptly and thoroughly.

## 2. Types of Misconduct

### 2.1 Harmful Behavior

We address:
- Harassment and cyberbullying
- Threats or intimidation
- Sexual harassment or unwanted advances
- Abuse or degrading treatment
- Discrimination based on protected characteristics

### 2.2 Exploitative Behavior

We prohibit:
- Scams or fraud
- Manipulation or coercion
- Exploitation of vulnerability
- Predatory behavior
- Financial exploitation

### 2.3 Illegal Activity

We report:
- Child exploitation
- Human trafficking
- Violent crime solicitation
- Drug trafficking
- Other serious crimes

## 3. Reporting Misconduct

### 3.1 In-App Reporting

Users can report misconduct through:
- Report button on user profiles
- Report button on posts/content
- Flag option in direct messages
- Block and report in comments

Steps:
1. Tap report/flag option
2. Select category of violation
3. Provide additional context
4. Submit report

### 3.2 Direct Report

Email: safety@rejectionhero.com

Include:
- User(s) involved
- Type of misconduct
- Specific examples or quotes
- Screenshots if applicable
- Date and time
- Your contact information

### 3.3 Emergency Reporting

For immediate threats or safety risks:
- Contact law enforcement (911 in US)
- Call National Crisis Line: 988
- Text Crisis Hotline: Text HOME to 741741

## 4. Investigation Process

### 4.1 Our Response

When we receive a report:
1. Acknowledge receipt within 24 hours
2. Assign to investigation team
3. Collect evidence and context
4. Interview relevant parties
5. Review chat logs or content
6. Consult with specialists if needed

### 4.2 Timeline

- Urgent cases (violence/exploitation): Within 24 hours
- Serious cases (harassment/threats): Within 48 hours
- Standard cases: Within 5 business days
- Complex cases: Within 10 business days

### 4.3 Investigation Scope

We examine:
- Relevant messages and content
- User history and patterns
- Context and circumstances
- Applicable policies and laws
- Third-party information if needed

## 5. Actions and Consequences

### 5.1 Possible Outcomes

- No action (insufficient evidence)
- Warning to accused user
- Content removal
- Temporary account suspension
- Permanent account termination
- Law enforcement referral

### 5.2 Escalation

Serious violations may involve:
- Preservation of evidence
- Cooperation with law enforcement
- Legal action
- Criminal investigation
- Victim support services

## 6. Protection for Reporters

We protect reporter identity:
- Reports are confidential
- Identity not disclosed to accused (if possible)
- No retaliation for good-faith reporting
- False reports may result in action
- Reporters protected under policy

## 7. Support Resources

For affected users, we provide:
- Blocking and muting tools
- Mental health resources
- Victim support services
- Privacy protection features
- Counseling referrals

Contact: support@rejectionhero.com

## 8. Transparency and Appeal

### 8.1 Transparency

When we take action:
- Accused user is notified
- Reason for action provided (when possible)
- Policy violated is explained
- Information may be limited for investigation

### 8.2 Appeals Process

Users can appeal within 30 days by:
1. Submitting written appeal
2. Providing new evidence
3. Explaining why decision was wrong
4. Requesting independent review

Decision within 10 business days.

## 9. Privacy in Investigations

We protect privacy while investigating:
- Limit information shared
- Secure evidence appropriately
- Comply with privacy laws
- Use information only for investigation
- Delete information after resolution

## 10. Cooperation with Law Enforcement

We will:
- Preserve evidence for lawful requests
- Comply with subpoenas and warrants
- Work with authorities on serious crimes
- Provide information within legal bounds
- Protect user privacy where permitted

## 11. Preventing Misconduct

We work to prevent harm through:
- AI-based detection systems
- Community reporting
- Moderation team monitoring
- Educational resources
- Safety guidelines

## 12. Special Protections for Minors

For users under 18:
- Enhanced monitoring of interactions
- Parental notification options
- Stricter action on predatory behavior
- Age-appropriate content enforcement
- Additional safety tools

## 13. Crisis Situations

If someone is in immediate danger:
- Call 911 (emergency services in US)
- Do not wait for our response
- Provide location to emergency services
- Contact crisis support services

## 14. Confidentiality Exceptions

We may disclose information:
- When required by law
- To prevent imminent harm
- With explicit consent
- To protect our rights
- In legal proceedings

## 15. False Reports

Making false reports:
- Violates our policies
- May result in account suspension
- Wastes resources
- Harms the accused

False reporters may face consequences.

## 16. Contact

Report misconduct:
Email: safety@rejectionhero.com
Urgent concerns: safety@rejectionhero.com (marked URGENT)
