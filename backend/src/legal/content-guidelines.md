# Content & Community Guidelines

**Effective Date:** November 18, 2025

## 1. Community Standards

Rejection Hero is committed to maintaining a positive, safe, and inclusive community. All users must respect these guidelines in their interactions, content, and behavior.

## 2. Prohibited Content

Users are prohibited from posting, sharing, or engaging in:

### 2.1 Illegal Content
- Content that violates local, state, national, or international law
- Promotion of illegal activities or substances
- Child exploitation material of any kind

### 2.2 Violent or Dangerous Content
- Graphic violence or gore
- Instructions for self-harm or suicide
- Threats of violence toward others
- Content glorifying or promoting violence

### 2.3 Harassment and Abuse
- Bullying or cyberbullying
- Hate speech or discrimination based on protected characteristics
- Sexual harassment or unwanted sexual content
- Doxxing or sharing private information
- Targeted harassment campaigns

### 2.4 Misinformation
- False health or medical information
- Dangerous misinformation that could cause harm
- Conspiracy theories presented as fact
- Deliberately misleading content

### 2.5 Adult Content
- Sexually explicit material
- Graphic sexual content or nude imagery
- Sex work solicitation
- Extreme adult content

### 2.6 Spam and Manipulation
- Repetitive or irrelevant content
- Commercial spam or advertising without permission
- Artificial engagement manipulation
- Phishing attempts or scams

## 3. Respectful Communication

All users should:
- Treat others with respect and dignity
- Assume good intentions
- Engage in constructive dialogue
- Accept differing perspectives
- Report inappropriate behavior

## 4. Content Moderation

We moderate content through:
- Automated detection systems
- Community reporting
- User complaints
- Regular review of trending content
- Manual review by moderation team

## 5. Consequences for Violations

Violations may result in:
- Content removal or hiding
- Warning or suspension notice
- Temporary account suspension
- Permanent account termination
- Legal action if applicable

## 6. Appeals Process

Users may appeal moderation decisions by:
- Submitting an appeal within 30 days
- Providing evidence or explanation
- Awaiting review by our appeals team
- Receiving a final decision within 7 days

## 7. Creator Responsibilities

Content creators agree to:
- Create original or properly licensed content
- Properly label potentially sensitive content
- Not impersonate others
- Respect intellectual property rights
- Follow all platform guidelines

## 8. User Safety

We are committed to:
- Blocking and reporting illegal content
- Protecting minors from harmful content
- Maintaining user anonymity when requested
- Providing blocking and reporting tools
- Investigating serious violations

## 9. Live Streaming Guidelines

During live streams, users must:
- Not stream adult or graphic content
- Ensure a safe environment
- Respect community guidelines
- Not engage in harassment
- Follow all platform rules

## 10. Accessibility and Inclusion

We strive to create an inclusive community:
- Encourage diverse perspectives
- Respect cultural differences
- Support accessibility features
- Prevent discrimination
- Foster belonging

## 11. Report a Violation

To report a violation, use the in-app reporting tool or contact:
Email: moderation@rejectionhero.com

## 12. Updates to Guidelines

We may update these guidelines periodically. Changes will be communicated to users.
