**Effective Date:** November 18, 2025

## 1. Copyright Compliance

Rejection Hero respects intellectual property rights and complies with the Digital Millennium Copyright Act (DMCA) and similar laws worldwide.

## 2. Copyright Policy

Users must not:
- Upload copyrighted content without authorization
- Use third-party music, images, or videos without license
- Claim ownership of others' content
- Infringe on intellectual property rights
- Violate publishing or distribution rights

## 3. DMCA Takedown Process

### 3.1 Filing a DMCA Notice

If you believe your copyright has been infringed, you may submit a DMCA takedown notice containing:

1. Your name, address, phone number, and email
2. Description of the copyrighted work
3. Location of infringing content (specific URL/identifiers)
4. Statement that you have a good faith belief the use is not authorized
5. Statement under penalty of perjury that information is accurate
6. Your physical or electronic signature

Submit to: dmca@rejectionhero.com

### 3.2 Designated Agent

Our DMCA agent is:
Rejection Hero Legal Department
Email: dmca@rejectionhero.com

### 3.3 Response Time

We will:
- Acknowledge receipt within 24 hours
- Investigate the claim
- Remove or disable infringing content
- Notify the content provider
- Process within 10 business days

## 4. Counter-Notice Process

### 4.1 Filing a Counter-Notice

If content was removed and you believe it was removed in error, submit a counter-notice with:

1. Your name, address, phone, and email
2. Identification of removed content and where it was located
3. Statement under penalty of perjury that removal was erroneous
4. Your physical or electronic signature
5. Statement that you consent to jurisdiction of appropriate court

Submit to: dmca@rejectionhero.com

### 4.2 Counter-Notice Effects

- We will forward your counter-notice to the original claimant
- Content may be restored after 10 business days
- The claimant may file a lawsuit to prevent restoration
- We will notify you of legal proceedings

## 5. Repeat Infringement Policy

### 5.1 Violations

- First violation: Warning
- Second violation: Content removal and account warning
- Third violation: Account suspension
- Pattern of violations: Permanent account termination

### 5.2 Termination

We will terminate repeat infringers' accounts in appropriate circumstances.
