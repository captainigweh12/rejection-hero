# Recording Consent & Release

**Effective Date:** November 18, 2025

## 1. Purpose

This document outlines the terms and conditions regarding audio and video recording within the Rejection Hero App, including live streams, video quests, and user-generated content.

## 2. Live Stream Recording

### 2.1 Recording Permission
By streaming on Rejection Hero, you consent to:
- Your stream being recorded and stored
- Recordings being made available to viewers
- Replay functionality after the stream ends
- Archival of streams for up to 90 days
- Analytics tracking of your streaming activity

### 2.2 Streamer Rights
- You retain copyright to your streamed content
- You grant Rejection Hero a license to store and display the stream
- You may request deletion of archived streams
- You are responsible for third-party content (music, images) in your stream

### 2.3 Viewer Recording
- Viewers may record streams only for personal use
- Commercial recording, editing, or distribution is prohibited
- Recorded content remains subject to copyright laws
- Unauthorized distribution may result in action

## 3. Video Quest Recording

### 3.1 Consent to Record
By participating in video quests, you consent to:
- Recording of your attempt
- Storage of video on our servers
- Sharing with specified audience (friends, public, private)
- Use for app analytics and improvement

### 3.2 Video Content Rights
- You retain ownership of your video
- You grant Rejection Hero a license to store and display
- You may delete your video at any time
- Deleted videos are removed within 7 days

## 4. Audio Recording

### 4.1 Voice Messages
- Voice messages may be recorded with your consent
- Audio is encrypted in transit and at rest
- Audio is deleted when you delete the message
- We do not use audio for training without explicit consent

### 4.2 Live Audio
- Audio in streams is recorded along with video
- Ambient audio is captured and stored
- Audio-only calls are not supported

## 5. Notification Requirements

Users must:
- Inform others when recording
- Obtain consent before recording others
- Display clear recording indicators
- Respect do-not-record requests
- Follow local recording consent laws

## 6. Consent and Privacy

### 6.1 Multi-Party Consent States
If you are in a jurisdiction requiring all-party consent for recording:
- Inform all participants you are recording
- Obtain explicit written consent
- Keep consent records
- Violations may result in legal liability

### 6.2 Data Protection
- Recorded content is protected by our privacy policy
- Data is encrypted and access-controlled
- Recordings are subject to retention schedules
- Deletion requests are honored

## 7. Prohibited Recording

Users must not:
- Record without consent (where required)
- Use recordings for harassment or blackmail
- Distribute recordings without permission
- Record in private spaces without notice
- Use recordings for commercial purposes without consent

## 8. Children's Recording

### 8.1 Parental Consent
Recording of minors (under 18) requires:
- Explicit parental consent
- Parent/guardian notification
- Age-appropriate content only
- Enhanced privacy protections

### 8.2 Minor Privacy
- We do not share minors' videos publicly
- Minors can limit who sees their content
- Parents can request content removal
- Compliance with COPPA requirements

## 9. Copyright and Intellectual Property

Users recording others agree to:
- Respect copyright laws
- Not use content for commercial purposes
- Attribute original creators
- Remove content upon request
- Comply with DMCA takedown notices

## 10. Recording Deletion

### 10.1 User Deletion
- You can delete your recordings anytime
- Deletion is permanent and cannot be undone
- Archived copies may take 7 days to remove
- Shared copies remain with recipients

### 10.2 Company Deletion
- We may delete recordings violating guidelines
- Offensive or harmful content will be removed
- Notice will be provided when possible
- Appeals process is available

## 11. Liability and Disclaimers

- Users are responsible for obtaining necessary consent
- Rejection Hero is not liable for unauthorized recording
- Users indemnify Rejection Hero for recording violations
- Unauthorized recording may result in legal action
- Platform is not responsible for third-party recording tools

## 12. Third-Party Tools

For screen recording or external capture:
- Comply with app terms and applicable laws
- Obtain all necessary consents
- Do not violate copyright or privacy
- Do not modify or redistribute app content
- Rejection Hero not liable for third-party tools

## 13. Enforcement

Violations may result in:
- Content removal
- Account warning or suspension
- Legal action by affected parties
- Criminal charges in applicable jurisdictions

We may update this policy. Changes are effective 30 days after notification.
